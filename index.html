PRODUCT LINE

Nurpur Original UHT Milk

Nurpur UHT milk is produced from freshly collected high quality full cream milk, is Ultra-High temperature treated and packed in both Tetra and Ecolean Packs

Nurpur Flavoured Milk

Flavoured Milk is a sweetened dairy product made with milk, sugar, flavourings, and permitted food colours. Its process involves Ultra-High temperature (UHT) to protect the product.

Nurpur Dairy Cream

Nurpur Cream is a smooth and delicious dairy cream, produced from fresh high quality milk, Ultra-High temperature treated and packed in Tetra packaging.

Nurpur Butter

Nurpur Butter is produced from fresh dairy cream with great taste due to high quality Nurpur Butter churn and hygienically packed for better protection and convenience.

Nurpur Cheese

Creamy and natural, Nurpur Cheese comes in three variants: Mozzarella, Cheddar and American Burger Slices. Each being uniquely delicious, no matter which one you try, Nurpur cheese will always leave you craving for more
